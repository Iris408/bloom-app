### External tester feedback — Reddit comments

**Source:** Reddit  
**Status:** Saved for future v1.6.0 / onboarding review  
**Priority:** Medium-high  
**Theme:** First-use experience, missed-day recovery, low-demand design

A tester suggested that Bloom’s first session should stay extremely small:

- One routine
- One focus block
- One end-of-day reflection

They also said Bloom should help users recover after missing a day rather than punish them with dashboards or streaks.

**Theme:** Home screen simplicity, low-demand design, missed-day recovery  
**Priority:** High  
**Status:** Saved for v1.6.0 / future onboarding and progress review

A Reddit tester said that apps in this space can lose users when they put too many widgets on the home screen. They prefer an app that gives them one tiny thing to do, and does not make them feel like they ruined the whole system if they skip a few days.

### Design takeaway

1. Bloom should avoid feeling like a traditional productivity dashboard. First-use guidance should stay low-demand, and future progress/streak features should focus on recovery, reassurance, and gentle continuation.

2. Bloom should avoid a crowded dashboard-style home screen. The first-use and daily-use experience should stay simple, focused, and recovery-friendly.
